Free Online Dice Roller

Roll virtual dice for games, D&D, or just for fun. Supports d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100.

  • Step 1: Select dice type (d6, d20, etc.)
  • Step 2: Choose how many dice

  • ✅ Roll up to 20 dice
  • ✅ Random & fair results
  • ✅ Fun animation
